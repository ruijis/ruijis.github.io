Problem Found

The downtown campus of my undergraduate university was too crowded. We lived in 8-people dormitory rooms, with only 1 big desk in the middle of each room. Most of the time, the desk was covered by daily necessities, so students didn’t have space to work or study.

Influenced by the Soviet model, most of the college dormitories in the downtown campus in China are tube-shaped apartments. With economic growth and rising standards of living, the crowded dormitory is considered anachronistic, inconvenient and inhumane.
